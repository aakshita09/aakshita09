Embroidered Saree



Hand-embroidered sarees are traditional Indian garments that are made by hand-stitching the fabric to create intricate patterns and designs. The art of hand embroidery has been passed down from generation to generation for centuries in India, with each region having its own unique style


Printed Saree



Printed Sarees are one of the most versatile and elegant pieces of clothing in the world. it cab be wear for any occasion, from casual to formal, and they come in a wide variety of styles and colors. One of the most popular types of sarees is the printed saree.


Embellished Saree



Embellished sarees are often referred to as Swarovski sarees, Stone Work Sarees, or simply embellished sarees. These names reflect the various techniques and materials used to create these stunning garments. Whether it is the sparkle of Swarovski crystals or the intricate work of stones, each embellished saree is a masterpiece that exudes beauty and refinement. The uniqueness of an embellished saree lies in its adaptability, it can easily mix both contemporary and traditional elements.


Handloom Saree




A handloom sari is often woven on a shuttle-pit loom made from ropes, wooden beams and poles. The shuttle is thrown from side-to-side by the weaver. Other weavers use a fly-shuttle loom which can produce different types of patterns. The saris can vary in size and quality.
